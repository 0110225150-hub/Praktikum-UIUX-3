LUNA "LOONY" LOVEGOOD

    Luna Lovegood, a fictional character from an adaptation film of J.K. Rowling's books, Harry Potter series. Here's some brief information about her:

  • Family: The Lovegoods
  • Born: 13 February 1981
  • Education: Hogwarts
  • House: Ravenclaw
  • Skills: Duelling, Care of Magical Creatures

An eccentric Ravenclaw student, member of Dumbledore’s Army and close friend of Harry Potter, Luna Lovegood might have been nicknamed ‘Loony’ for her quirky ways, but she was in fact often one of Harry’s most clear-sighted friends.

Luna was undeterred in her belief in herself and her friends but with a father who edited the notoriously unreliable Quibbler magazine and a unique personal style, including homemade dirigible plum earrings, Luna had no choice but to stand out from the crowd. Her courage, loyalty and kindness earned her place in Harry’s circle of most trusted friends.
